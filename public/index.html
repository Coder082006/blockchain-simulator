📚 Learn Mode (Step-by-Step)

Follow these steps to understand how blockchain works:

  1. Step 1: Queue a transaction

    Enter a sender, recipient, and amount in the "Add Transaction" section below, then click "Queue Transaction".

  2. Step 2: Mine the block

    Enter your miner address and click "Mine Block". Watch the nonce increment as it finds a valid hash.

  3. Step 3: See the block chain

    A new block appears below with the transaction. Notice how blocks are linked via their hashes.

  4. Step 4: Tamper with a block

    Scroll down and click "⚠️ Tamper with this block" on a mined block. Watch the chain break!
